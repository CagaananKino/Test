Lesson 1: Anatomy

Anatomy is not limited to just humans. Cats, Dogs, or even inanimate objects can be drawn with anatomy. Everything are shapes and we just need to disect and find them out to understand.

Human Body

The Human body consists of alot of tiny intricate details that is hard to grasp when drawing. Although we can start at simple shapes to really get in the feel of the drawing at hand. Note that you are not forced to follow these, you may even create your own anatomy as your basis!

Anatomy Picture

Torso

There are three sections for the Torso which are The Chest, Mid and Crotch

Chest

Generally a rectangular prism that is more rounded like oval shapes

Mid

Generally just a connecting tool usually consisting of just a square

Crotch

Like a square with its bottom corners cut like those of underpants!

Limbs

Legs

Arm

Hands

A square with circular spheres on where the digits are usually placed.

Feet

These are tad bit complicated, usually used with organic shapes with a little formal shapes
