Shapes are your backbone on creating your drawings into somewhat of a 3d object in a 2d plane. This is an Illusion to some giving your drawings some height, depth, and width. This may alsobe used to determine shadows, lighting, etc. Overall shapes give you a more in depth figure for your drawings.

Kinds Of Shapes

There are 2 kinds of shape namely the one is simplfied the other!

Geometric Shapes

These shapes are known to be the basics in drawing, namely the ones used for math too and are in a fixed straight shape!

Organic Shapes

These shapes are known to be the free formed Asymetric shapes known for its unpredicatable curves and sizes!
