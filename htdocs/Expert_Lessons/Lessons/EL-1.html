Types of shading

There are 2 types of shading, each having their own uses and styles!

Cel Shading

Sectioned Shadows that has no smooth transition to the other shadow

Soft Shading

Sectioned shadow where it is now a smooth transition to the other shadows

Shading on Object

There are 3 types of shading that affects the object itself and itself only!

Shadows

Typically your side where light cannot touch the object giving the illusion of a darker color

Central Light

Typically your side where light can touch the object giving the illusion of a lighter color

Highlights

Typically the actual light source bouncing off the object towards the viewer

Shading Around the object

There are 3 types of shading that affects the surrounding area by the object!

Cast Shadow

The light being blocked by the object giving the illusion of shadows being Casted from the object

Reflected Light

Bouncing light that acts upon surfaces. Light bounces on everything, so take upon the view of the surface

Occlusion Shadow

It counters Reflected light as the object being closer to the surface adds more of a dark tone towards it
